Server Information

GrapeSMP is a fun, engaging, and community-driven Minecraft Earth SMP server that offers players a unique twist on traditional survival gameplay. The server is set on a massive and detailed Earth map, allowing players to explore real-world continents, build towns and countries, and experience Minecraft like never before. Whether you're starting a small village in Europe, building a city in North America, or dominating trade in Asia, GrapeSMP gives you the tools to shape the world around you. What really makes GrapeSMP stand out are the special plugins we use, which are custom-made to add new layers of excitement and creativity. From land claiming and custom jobs to a player-run economy and teleportation systems, these features make the server more immersive and enjoyable for everyone. GrapeSMP runs in seasons such as Season 1, Season 2, Season 3 and so on, with each one bringing fresh opportunities, new features, and exciting events to keep the experience dynamic and ever-evolving. With each season, players have a chance to make history and leave their mark on the world. We are also always on the lookout for new staff members who are responsible, friendly, and ready to help grow and support the community.
